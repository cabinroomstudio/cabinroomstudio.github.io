Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Weighly, developed by Cabin Room Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data We Collect

Weighly stores your weight entries (weight value and date) locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share this data with any third party.

Feedback & Bug Reports

If you choose to submit feedback or a bug report through the app, tapping the feedback button will open your email app with our address pre-filled. No data is automatically collected or transmitted by Weighly.

Data Storage

All weight data is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data.

Third Party Services

No third party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are used in this app.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at cabinroomstudio42@gmail.com
